"Defining relationship" Performing Art

1. Two people do rock-scissors-paper.
2. The one who lost put his/her one hand on the table and the one who win put his/her one hand on the hand on the table.
3. The one who lost put his/her other hand on the winner's hand and winner put his/her other hand on the pile of hand.
4. Two people tries to pull out one hand at a time on the pile of time and tries to put both hands on the other's both hands.
5. When both hands are on the other's, stays like that for 10 seconds.
6. Then the one who touches table starts to put his/her hand on the pile and game goes on and one.

This performing art is based on Hegel's "Master-Slave" theory
